i-Transform is an experiential learning program to prepare you for successful delivery of complex projects using new technologies. It is an additional channel to help you learn the latest technologies in a self-study model with the prime objective of upskilling and providing you opportunities for growth and career advancement.

                                                                             

This program is open and applicable to all employees, irrespective of whether you are on a project or on the bench. Anyone interested in upskilling and investing in their careers are welcome to participate. Team members who are in between projects or have been working on their current engagements for 18 months or more are highly encouraged to build iTransform into their learning objectives.
